# Code of Conduct

Aramis is maintained as a professional engineering project. Keep discussions technical, respectful, and focused on improving the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, factual, and respectful.
- Keep reports and reviews actionable.
- Assume operational safety matters.
- Avoid sharing secrets, private infrastructure details, or confidential logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing credentials, tokens, private keys, or sensitive server details.
- Spam, low-effort noise, or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues; block accounts; or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
